**Last Updated:** December 22, 2024  
**Effective Date:** December 22, 2024

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Genki TCG ("we," "our," or "us"). We operate the Genki TCG mobile application (the "App"), a tournament management platform designed exclusively for Trading Card Game (TCG) communities at local game stores.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and share your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains our practices in compliance with:
- Apple App Store Guidelines
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information:**
- Email address (from Discord OAuth)
- Display name and username
- Profile avatar/profile picture
- Discord user ID and username (for authentication)

**Tournament Activity Data:**
- Event registrations and check-ins
- Match results and game outcomes
- Decklist submissions (deck names and card lists)
- Tournament standings and placements
- Entry withdrawal/drop history

**Credits and Transaction Data:**
- In-app credit balance (virtual currency for tournament entry)
- Transaction history (credits earned from prizes, spent on entry fees)
- Prize distribution records

**Communication Data:**
- Push notification preferences
- In-app notification history
- Support inquiries (if submitted)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Device and Technical Information:**
- Device type, model, and manufacturer
- Operating system and version (iOS/Android)
- Unique device identifiers (for push notifications only)
- App version and build number
- Mobile network carrier information

**Usage Analytics:**
- Screens and features accessed within the App
- Tournament events browsed and joined
- Session duration and frequency
- Button interactions and navigation patterns
- Timestamps of App usage

**Performance and Diagnostic Data (via Sentry):**
- Application crash reports and error logs
- Stack traces and exception data
- Device state at time of error
- API response times and network errors
- These logs are anonymized and do not contain personally identifiable information

### 2.3 Information from Third-Party Sources

**Discord OAuth Integration:**
When you log in via Discord, we receive:
- Discord User ID (unique identifier)
- Discord Username and Display Name
- Email address associated with your Discord account
- Avatar image URL

We do NOT receive:
- Your Discord password
- Your Discord messages or chat history
- Your Discord server memberships
- Your Discord friends list

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data as follows:

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account Information | Until account deletion + 30 days |
| Tournament History | Indefinitely (anonymized after account deletion) |
| Match Results | Indefinitely (part of tournament records) |
| Transaction History | 7 years (legal/financial requirements) |

| Crash Reports | 90 days |

After account deletion, we may retain anonymized tournament records for the integrity of historical competition data.

## 7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

**Technical Safeguards:**
- All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS 1.2+
- Passwords (if any) are hashed using bcrypt with secure salt
- Database access is restricted and monitored
- API endpoints are protected with JWT-based authentication
- Refresh tokens are securely managed with automatic expiration

**Organizational Safeguards:**
- Access to user data is limited to authorized personnel only
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- Prompt patching of security vulnerabilities
- Secure development practices

**Important Note:** No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

### 8.1 All Users

**Access Your Data:** View your profile, tournament history, and credit balance directly in the App.

**Update Your Data:** Edit your display name and notification preferences in App settings.

**Delete Your Account:** Request account deletion by contacting support@genkitcg.com. Upon deletion:
- Your account and personal data will be removed within 30 days
- Tournament records may be anonymized rather than deleted to preserve competition integrity
- Credit balances cannot be refunded after deletion

**Manage Push Notifications:** Control notification preferences in the App or disable them via your device settings.

### 8.2 California Residents (CCPA Rights)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have the right to:
- **Know:** What personal information we collect, use, and share
- **Delete:** Request deletion of your personal information
- **Non-Discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

We do NOT sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact privacy@genkitcg.com.

### 8.3 European Residents (GDPR Rights)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate personal data
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction:** Request limitation of processing
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format
- **Object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent for processing where applicable

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@genkitcg.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
