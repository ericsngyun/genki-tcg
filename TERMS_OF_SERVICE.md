## 2. Service Description

Genki TCG is a tournament management platform designed exclusively for local game store Trading Card Game (TCG) communities. The Service enables:

- **Tournament Participation:** Register for, check into, and participate in organized play events
- **Match Tracking:** View pairings, record results, and monitor tournament standings
- **Player Rankings:** Track Glicko-2 based ratings and compete on leaderboards
- **Credits System:** Manage virtual currency for tournament entry fees and prize distribution
- **Event Notifications:** Receive updates about rounds, pairings, and results
- **Supported Games:** One Piece TCG, Azuki TCG, and Riftbound

The Service is intended to support organized play at affiliated local game stores ("Organizations").

## 6. Tournament Rules and Policies

### 6.1 General Tournament Conduct

- All participants must adhere to fair play standards
- Tournament organizers have authority over all in-event decisions
- Match results reported in the App are considered official
- Disputes must be raised immediately with tournament staff

### 6.2 Registration and Check-In

- Registering for an event does not guarantee a spot (capacity limits may apply)
- You must check in before the event start time as specified
- Failure to check in may result in forfeiture of your spot
- Entry fees paid through credits may not be refundable for no-shows

### 6.3 Match Reporting

- Players are expected to report match results accurately
- False or disputed results should be reported to tournament staff immediately
- Tournament organizers may override reported results when necessary
- Intentional misreporting is grounds for immediate disqualification

### 6.4 Dropping from Tournaments

- You may withdraw from a tournament at any time before it begins
- Once a tournament is in progress, dropping affects standings and future pairings
- Entry fees paid for dropped tournaments may not be refundable

### 6.5 Decklists

- Tournaments may require decklist submission
- Decklists must be submitted before the tournament lock deadline
- Playing with an illegal or undeclared deck may result in penalties
- Decklists may be shared with tournament staff for verification

### 6.6 Player Ratings

- Ratings are calculated using the Glicko-2 algorithm
- Lifetime and seasonal ratings are tracked separately
- Rating manipulation through collusion or intentional losses is prohibited
- Ratings are displayed publicly on leaderboards
