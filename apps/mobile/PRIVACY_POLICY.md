# Privacy Policy for Genki TCG

**Last Updated:** December 20, 2025

## Introduction

Genki TCG ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Genki TCG mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Discord Account Information:**
- Email address
- Discord username and ID
- Discord avatar (profile picture)

When you log in via Discord OAuth, we receive this information from Discord to create and maintain your account.

### Automatically Collected Information

**Usage Data:**
- Tournament participation history
- Match results
- Credit transactions
- Leaderboard rankings
- Event check-ins

**Device Information:**
- Device ID (for push notifications)
- Operating system and version
- App version

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

1. **Account Management**
   - Creating and maintaining your user account
   - Authenticating your identity via Discord OAuth

2. **App Functionality**
   - Tournament registration and management
   - Displaying real-time pairings and standings
   - Tracking your tournament performance and rankings
   - Managing your credits wallet
   - Sending push notifications for pairings, results, and updates

3. **Analytics**
   - Understanding how users interact with the App
   - Improving app performance and user experience
   - Identifying and fixing bugs

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

**With Other Users:**
- Your Discord username and avatar are visible to other tournament participants
- Your tournament results and rankings are publicly displayed on leaderboards

**With Third-Party Services:**
- **Discord:** For authentication purposes
- **Expo Push Notifications:** For sending tournament updates

**Legal Requirements:**
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Secure authentication via Discord OAuth
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and logging

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email address below.
